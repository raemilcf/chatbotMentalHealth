AI for Emotional Wellbeing

Mental health is a critical aspect of overall well-being, yet access to mental health support remains limited for many. Artificial intelligence and natural language processing have the potential to provide accessible and scalable mental health support.

Objective

Develop an NLP-based chatbot to provide emotional support and enhance mental well-being.

Methodology

A wide range of NLP techniques are utilized to handle user inputs and provide more accurate responses.

Design

A simple and user-friendly design that processes user input and works on a knowledge base to provide the best user feedback

Mental Health Chatbot

User-focused design for personalized help and crisis intervention, improving emotional well-being.
